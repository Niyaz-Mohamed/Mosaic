Things to note

When using the editor, using smaller images is highly encouraged as the changes that appear on them when converted are far more obvious than in larger images even at lower degrees of pixelation

Convertion

Mosiac's editor currently supports features to change any image into pixel art with varying degrees of pixelation, greyscaling any images you create, and changing the width (and scaling the height approppriately as well) of the pixel art.

Downloading

Images cannot be downloaded directly from the editor. To download the pixel art, first save the art to your personal library, and downloading it from there instead
